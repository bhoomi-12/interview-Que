Types of directives in Angular

There are 3 types of directives:
structural directive,
attribute directives and
component directives

so structural directives are :-

NGif , ng4 , ng-switch
so these are the examples for structural directive will change the structure of the Doc..

Attribute directives are NG style , NG class.

And component is also one type of directive
but only the difference is component will have template means view part,
and directive will have only the logic .
